Health2You Privacy Policy

Chips Studio · Last updated: 2026-06-29

Health2You is designed as a local-first wellness app. This policy describes what data the app uses and what is not collected by Chips Studio.

Health Data

Health2You can read selected Apple Health / HealthKit data only after you grant permission. This may include sleep, daylight, activity, HRV, resting heart rate, and related wellness summaries.

Apple Health data is read only on device. Health2You processes this information on your device to generate sleep rhythm summaries, local rhythm scores, trend views, 10-day plans, and report exports.

What We Do Not Collect

Chips Studio does not collect raw HealthKit samples from Health2You.

Health data is not uploaded to Chips Studio.

Health2You does not collect:

  • Raw HealthKit samples
  • HealthKit source names or raw category records
  • Account credentials
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Third-party tracking identifiers
  • Analytics events
  • Server-side health profiles
  • LLM prompts containing health data

Local Reports

Reports are generated locally on your device. The app may store derived report summaries locally so you can reopen the last report or view report history. These local files are stored on device and can be replaced or cleared from the app.

Report exports are created only when you choose to export or share them.

Accounts, Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Health2You does not require an account.

Health2You does not include ads.

Health2You does not include analytics.

Health2You does not use third-party tracking.

Subscriptions

Health2You Pro subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Subscription purchase and billing information is managed by Apple. Health2You uses StoreKit transaction status to unlock Pro features.

Medical Disclaimer

Health2You is for wellness and lifestyle reflection only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It does not provide medication, supplement, or melatonin recommendations. If you have health concerns, consult a qualified healthcare professional.
